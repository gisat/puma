PUMA project

The PUMA project has been made by Czech Republic based GISAT company. The main aim of this project has been to develop a Platform for Urban Management and Analysis (PUMA).  

Beside this software development, the PUMA project also involves satellite Imagery based mapping. Land cover and change in five metropolitan areas in eastern and south eastern Asia has been mapped in frame of this project. Details on this mapping are specified in the Interim report of the PUMA project.

More information on the PUMA project is available on the project website.

The Platform for Urban Management and Analysis (PUMA) has been developed by the GISAT company. It has concentrated on the development of a supporting web platform to enable staff in the East Asia and Pacific region working on the metropolitan and city scale to derive a standard preliminary analysis that better informs about the priorities and facilitates the cross-sector coordination of their investment and advisory activities.

The platform and its outputs shall help clients develop a shared understanding of the long-term spatial, economic and environmental implications of land use, spatial development and infrastructure investment choices.

Besides the software platform development, the PUMA project also involved satellite imagery-based mapping as part of the assignment for satellite imagery-processing consultation. Land cover and change in five metropolitan areas in eastern and south eastern Asia has been mapped in the frame of this project. Details on this mapping are specified in detail in the PUMA documentation.
